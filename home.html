About AIGST-2027

The International Conference on AI Innovations in Green and Sustainable Technologies (AIGST-2027) is an esteemed global event dedicated to advancing research and innovation in Artificial Intelligence in sustainable and environmentally friendly technologies. This conference aims to bring together researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and students to discuss and promote the development and implementation of green technologies that can address the pressing environmental challenges of our time.

Conference Objectives

  • To provide a premier platform for presenting the latest research findings and technological advancements of AI in green and sustainable technologies.
  • To foster interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge exchange among experts from diverse fields including civil, mechanical, electrical, electronics, computer science, and applied sciences.
  • To explore practical solutions and strategies for integrating sustainable practices in various sectors.
  • To engage in meaningful dialogue on policy-making and regulatory frameworks that support environmental sustainability.

Key Themes

  • Artificial Intelligence for Green Computing and Sustainable Information Technology.
  • Smart Grids and Renewable Energy Integration Using Intelligent Systems.
  • Machine Learning for Energy-Efficient Circuit Design and Optimization.
  • Intelligent Technologies for Smart and Sustainable Construction.
  • Energy-Efficient Mechanical Systems Through Intelligent Optimization.
  • Nanotechnology for Sustainable Applications Using Intelligent Algorithms.
  • Intelligent Tools for Advanced Mathematical Modeling of Environmental Systems.
  • Intelligent Solutions for Biochemical Processes in Environmental Applications.
  • Quantum Computing and Intelligent Optimization for Energy-Efficient Solutions.

Springer Publication

Selected accepted papers will be submitted for publication in Springer Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS), subject to Springer approval and quality requirements.

Highlights of AIGST 2027

  • Keynote Addresses: Insights from leading global experts and innovators in green technologies.
  • Technical Sessions: Presentation of peer-reviewed research papers, case studies, and innovative projects.
  • Workshops and Tutorials: Hands-on sessions and in-depth discussions on specialized topics and emerging technologies.
  • Panel Discussions: Conversations on current trends, future challenges, sustainability goals, and policy implications.
  • Exhibitions and Demonstrations: Showcasing the latest products, technologies, and solutions from industry leaders, researchers, and startups.

Who Should Attend:

  • Researchers and academicians focused on sustainable and green technologies.
  • Industry professionals and practitioners seeking to implement sustainable solutions.
  • Policymakers and government officials involved in environmental regulations and sustainability initiatives.
  • Students and early-career professionals eager to learn and network with experts in the field.
